UMAI RESTBENT is a merk that serves a fusion of Japanese and Javanese cuisine. Embark on a culinary adventure to the crossroads of Japan and Indonesia at UMAI. It was established in 2020 and is located in the Purwakarta. The menu features classic Javanese dishes, such as krengseng and rica-rica, as well as authentic Japanese favorites, such as chicken katsu and beef teriyaki.
